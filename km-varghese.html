K. M. Varghese

c. 1880s – 1960s  ·  Mavelikara, Kerala

Painter, author, and chronicler of Kerala's Syrian Christian heritage. K. M. Varghese was a self-taught artist whose portraits graced churches, colleges, and royal households across Travancore. He was equally prolific as a writer, contributing works of history, fiction, and literary criticism to the Malayalam press over five decades.
