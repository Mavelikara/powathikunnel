About the Powathikunnel Family

A heritage spanning nearly two millennia — from the first Christian converts of Kerala to 773 documented members across 12 generations, settled across India and the world.

✝️ Origins & Lineage

AD 52
The First Converts
St. Thomas reached Kerala and converted several Brahmin families near Palayoor, Chavakkad. Among them were the Pakalomattom, Sankarapuri, Kali and Kalikavu families — the earliest ancestors of our lineage.
AD 800s
Kuravilangadu Settlement
The Pakalomattom family settled at Kuravilangadu and divided into two branches — Vadakkedam (north) and Thekkedam (south). The Vadakkedam line is the direct ancestor of the Powathikunnel name.
AD 1800
The Five Brothers
Five brothers of the Thekkedam family fled Mannanam after a confrontation with a king's servant. Each settled in a different place — Kottayam, Kattachira, Thuruthy, and Changanachery — founding several family branches.
AD 1719
Mallappally Settlement
Varghese Vazhappallil, descended from the Changanachery brother, moved to Mallappally at the wish of the local ruler Poovana Panicker. He was given a place called Bhagavathikunnu — from which the name Powathikunnel derives.
AD 1806
Mavelikara Branch Founded
Mallappally Varghese's grandson moved to Mavelikara, later joined by Ulahannan Ittyavirah from the Kattachira line. Together they founded the Mavelikara Kudumbayogam — the basis of this family directory.

🌿 The Six Mallappally Branches

From the Mallappally settlement, six main family branches spread across Kerala and beyond:

Powathikunnel

The founding branch, named after Bhagavathikunnu

Palackkamannil

One of the earliest offshoots from Mallappally

Payyampallil

Branch that later moved to Edathua via Veliyathu

Vattasseril

Family of Orthodox Metropolitan H.H. Geevarghese Mar Deevannasios

Thuruthyvallil

Settled in the Thuruthy area of central Kerala

Vazhayil

One of the prominent Mallappally family lines

📚 About This Directory

This family directory was compiled by Jacob Oommen (Legy), Gen 10, as part of an ongoing effort to digitise, document and preserve the complete history of the Powathikunnel family for future generations.

The directory currently covers 773 members across 12 generations from two family trees — the Mallappally Varghese tree and the Ulahannan Ittyavirah tree. Data has been sourced from the Powathikunnel Kudumbayogam family history books and family records.

This is a living document. If you notice any errors, have additional information, or would like to add family members, please use the How To Use tab to submit a suggestion.

How To Use This Directory

A quick guide to finding family members, navigating the tree, and suggesting additions or corrections.

📋 Directory Tab

1

Browse or Search

Type any name, location, or pet name in the search bar to find a family member instantly. Use the dropdowns to filter by Generation, Gender, or Family Tree.

2

Click a Card to View Profile

Click any member card to open their full profile — showing their biography, parents, spouses, date of birth, location, and children.

3

Add or Edit a Profile

Hover over any card to reveal the ✎ edit button. Click it to correct a name, add a date, or update any details. Changes save automatically and are visible to all family members.

4

Add a Photo

Open any profile and click 📷 Add Photo. You can upload one or several photos. They are stored securely and visible to everyone. You can also remove a photo using the 🗑 button.

5

Add a New Member

Click the ＋ Add Member button at the top. Fill in as much or as little as you know — even just a name and generation is helpful. The new member will appear immediately.

🌳 Family Tree Tab

1

Choose a Tree

Select either the Mallappally Varghese Tree or the Ulahannan Ittyavirah Tree from the dropdown. The tree starts from the root ancestor.

2

Navigate by Clicking

Click any ancestor card on the left to go up the tree. Click any descendant card on the right to go deeper. The focused person always appears in the centre with full details.

3

Jump to Anyone

Use the search box in the tree toolbar to jump directly to any family member by name — no need to navigate step by step.

4

Breadcrumb Trail

The gold breadcrumb bar at the top shows your path from the root. Click any name in it to jump back up the chain instantly.
